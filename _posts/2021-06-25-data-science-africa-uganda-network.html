Two members of the Lab are on the interim coordination team for setting up DSA Uganda network whose mission is an all-inclusive network of data scientists, enthusiasts, and organisations inspired to grow data science skills and their usage to contribute to Uganda's development aspirations, as started in the National Development Plan III (NDPIII). The network also has clear objectives and proposed activities for details visit the website: http://www.datascienceafrica.org/dsaUganda/ The network has so far organised three pre-launch events the details of follow in the next part of his annex.

Events

1st DSA Uganda Pre launch Event

Date: April 30, 2021

Event link: http://www.datascienceafrica.org/dsaUganda/events/pre-launch.html

The first pre launch event was organised around the following objectives:

  • To bring together partners working in the data science space within the country (Academia, UN, civil society, private sector (banking, insurance, fintech, utility, social security), government [local, and central];
  • To showcase local case studies and potential of data science and increase its awareness;
  • To increase interest and ownership in data science among stakeholders;
  • To Communicate the agenda of the local chapter so that the people can be aware of what is available on offer and simulate discussion on the partnership going forward.

2nd DSA Uganda Pre launch Event

Date: May 28, 2021

Event link: http://www.datascienceafrica.org/dsaUganda/events/pre-launch-2.html

The second pre launch event was themed around opportunities in, for and around data science in Uganda and attracted presentations from government institutions, policy makers, industry experts, research institutes, academic institutions, entrepreneurs, among others giving examples of opportunities for and around data science in Uganda. 346 individuals applied for the event, with applicants from 5 new districts of Mbale, Kayunga, Hoima, Soroti and Kween.

Contributors to the second pre launch event were; Ministry of ICT Uganda, NITAU, ClinicPesa, Pollicy, Innovation Village Uganda, FINCA Bank and Data Science Africa Network.

3rd DSA Uganda Pre launch Event

Date: June 25, 2021

Event link: http://www.datascienceafrica.org/dsaUganda/events/pre-launch-3.html

The third pre launch event was themed around Showcasing data science research and projects in Universities. The event was centered around student research and this is basically to encourage and foster the growth of AI research at universities through networkings and collaborations, mentorships and supervisions from experts in industry or even other universities.

Contributors to the second pre launch event were from six Universities, 5 Ugandan including; Makerere University; Uganda Technical And Management University; Gulu University; Mbarara University of Science and Technology; and Busitema University, then Rhodas University in South Africa. The presenters included two University lecturers and six students.

To date, data science Africa Uganda chapter has attracted participation from 388 students, researchers and professionals that make up the local community of the Uganda Network.

The map in Figure 2, shows participants residential/work distributions among the 15 sub regions of Uganda. Because of the 42 day lock down the launch that was due in July is now postponed to a later month, but pre-launch events organised by the regions will start this month, with the Northern region ( 4 subregions of West Nile, Achioli, Lango and Karamoja) organising this month.
